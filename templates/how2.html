How to Use

Generating a Schedule

  1. Select the date that you took notes on the subject, or approximate
  2. Name Your Topic to Study
  3. If Necessary, Briefly Describe Your Topic
  4. Choose If You Want to Study on Weekends
  5. Select a Time to Study
  6. Click The 'Send It' Button & Download the .ics file
  7. If no data is entered, example data will be generated.
  8. To easily clear your input, select the 'Clear Form' option, and click the 'Send It' button

Importing to Google Calender

  1. In Google Calender, click the 'Settings' icon in the top right
  2. Click 'Settings'
  3. On the left side, toward the middle, click 'Import & Export'
  4. Click 'Select File'
  5. Select the new .ics file
  6. To switch calenders when importing, change the 'Add to Calender' value
  7. Click 'Import'
