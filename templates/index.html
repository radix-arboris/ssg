How does it work?

    Generating a Schedule...

  1. Name Your Topic to Study
  2. Briefly Describe Your Topic
  3. Select Whether or Not to Allow Studying on Weekends
  4. Select a Time to Study
  5. Click The 'Send It' Button & Download the .ics file
  6. If no data is entered, example data will be generated.
  7. To easily clear your input, select the 'Clear Form' option, and click the 'Send It' button

    8. Importing to Google Calender...

  8. In Google Calender, click the 'Settings' icon in the top right
  9. Click 'Settings'
  10. On the left side, toward the middle, click 'Import & Export'
  11. Click 'Select File'
  12. Select the new .ics file
  13. To switch calenders when importing, change the 'Add to Calender' value
  14. Click 'Import'
